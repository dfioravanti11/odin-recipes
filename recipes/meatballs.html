Meatballs

An old family recipe. Serve alone or with meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 cup pecorino romano cheese
  • 1 cup seasoned (or plain) breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions

Step 1

Add all ingredients to a large bowl and mix thoroughly by hand.

Step 2

Form meat conglomerate into medium sized balls and add to hot oiled skillet. Cook evenly on all sides till brown. Serve with marinara.

Enjoy!
